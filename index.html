Guangchao Geng

Ph.D. Candidate in Electrical Engineering at Zhejiang University

Welcome to Guangchao Geng's homepage!

Guangchao Geng is a Ph.D. candidate of the College of Electrical Engineering at Zhejiang University, China. He received his B.E. with Chu Kochen Honor in Zhejiang Univerisity, China, in 2009. He worked as a graduate research assistant from 2009 to 2012. His advisor is Dr. Quanyuan Jiang.

Currently, he is a visiting Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Iowa State University, United States, sponsored by China Scholarship Council. He is collaborating with Dr. V.Ajjarapu, working on a research project on stability constrained optimal power flow.

His research interests include power system stability and control, numerical analysis and optimization, and high performance computing.
